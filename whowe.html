Who we are:

Hi! My name is Sai Nay (online name for privacy concerns) and I'm the coder for this website. I'm a teenager living in Virginia and I like watching anime and learning javascript in my free time. I will not disclose the name of my friend that researched and found the sources about the candidates

Last month or so, I decided to search up "news today!". After some careless scrolling, I saw that the gubernatorial election was coming up soon. And I was simply shocked. I knew nothing about the candidates or anything that was happening. I asked my dad, he didn't even know the names of the candidates. I asked my sister, she couldn't come up with anything either. It was then when I realized, many people want to know about the election, they're just not as unemployed as me, meaning that they simply don't have enough time, and I get it. Time in such a fast-paced society flies faster than a hurricane breeze. Not everyone can catch up on election news or find complete and easy to acsess information.

It was then when I decided that I wanted to make a website to teach people about our local election in Virginia. Teaming up with a friend, we made this website (and so sorry for publishing it so late)

We are not professional policy analyzers in any way, shape, or form, just teenagers that want to help out their local community. We apologize in advance for any bias or incorrect information that we may have presented. This website was not made to discredit, insult, or defame any candidate, party member, or political figure. We encourage readers to verify statements from official campaign statements. This website is independently made and is not affiliated with any candidate, campaign, or organization.
